I solve problems in three simple steps.

01
Finding the best solution"The best solution is oftentimes the simplest"
Through careful analysis and methodical problem-solving, finding the best solution to fit all demands is what I excel at. 
02
Carving a solid base "Without a strong foundation, you will have trouble creating anything of value"
When I start a project, I always keep in mind the final objective, and the details that will come later. It's always better to have a flexible, robust base when creating anything, so that later progress is not hindered by a lack in the earlier stages.
03
Sculpting out the details"The details are not the details, they make the design"
The final step is to connect all the components together, and then polish, shape, and finalise the project. This step is oftentimes either too emphasised, or mostly forgotten, but it is just as important as the other two.
